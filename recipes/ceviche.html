Ceviche

Description

This is an amazing and proper flag recipe from Peru, with is prepared with fresh fish and some seafood

It's a recognized plate around the world which one make falls in love to anyone who try it

Ingredients

  • 12 lemons
  • 1kg fish
  • 1 onion cutted
  • 1 yellow chili

Steps

  1. First we need mix fish with lemon juice
  2. Then we add yellow chili
  3. Finally we mix it with onion, and we let fish rest for 15min
  4. And you have your ceviche, DONE! Enjoy it!
